PRO User Accounts

In order to receive in-call payments or charge customers for your time you will need to sign up for a PRO account. You can sign up from the profile tab.

Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • A business or individual bank account based in the United States
  • Either your Debit Card info or Bank Account & Routing Number
  • Last 4 digits of your Social Security number
  • A description about the services you will provide

Once you have signed up for a PRO account a PRO icon will appear next to your name.

There are 2 types of calls you can make on MOTI

1

Charge for your time

You can set your own per minute rate that is presented to your customer when you call them. The customer will see the rate and be able to set a time limit on the call so they know they won't be charged more than a certain amount. At the end of the time limit they can extend the length of the call. Either party can stop the call at any time and call times are rounded up to the upcoming minute.

2

Cover the call fee

If you choose to cover the cost you will be charged 10¢ per minute (USD) for the call, with a minimum 50¢ fee. This mode is great for professionals and retailers that are looking to sell goods or services during the call.

All transactions that occur on MOTI are handled by Stripe. Stripe will verify you by the information you provide, so it's important you provide accurate information, otherwise you will not be able to receive any money into your bank account.

Once you have been verified by Stripe the first payment you receive to your bank account may take 7-10 business days. After that you should receive money within 2 business days of a successful charge.

Stripe prohibits certain types of use on their platform. Please refer to their Prohibited Business page before you create a PRO account.

Video Chat Recording

MOTI offers a unique feature that lets users record the video chat as it happens directly to their phone.

Imagine being tutored for an upcoming test and not having to write everything down. Record the lesson and refer to it later so you can spend more valuable time asking questions and getting answers.

If you're a lawyer you can record a deposition from your client while you're getting paid for your services.

Maybe you just want to record you and your friend talking about whatever, or talking to your loved one so you can always remember that moment when you couldn't be there.

The possibilities are endless. We'd love to see what you record if there's something you want to share with the world. Post it on Instagram and tag @motiapp.

We're all about consent, so when you hit the record button the other user has to grant you permission. Record indicators will appear on the screen so both parties know when recording has started and completed.
